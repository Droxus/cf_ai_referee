# AI Football Referee Prompts

- Ask in-game questions: e.g., “What happens if the ball crosses the goal line?”
- Ask about football terms: e.g., "What is offside?”
- Hypothetical football scenarios: e.g., “If Arsenal wins the Premier League, can they play in the Champions League next season?”
- Ask about referee decisions: e.g., “Should this be a penalty if the defender handles the ball inside the box?”
- Ask about match situations: e.g., “What happens if the goalkeeper steps outside the penalty area while holding the ball?”
- Ask about rules clarifications: e.g., “Can a player be substituted after receiving a yellow card?”
- Ask about league-specific rules: e.g., “How many substitutes are allowed in a Premier League match?”
